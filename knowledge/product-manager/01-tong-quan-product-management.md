# Tổng Quan Product Management

## Product Manager là gì?

Product Manager (PM) là người chịu trách nhiệm về **thành công của sản phẩm**. PM là bridge giữa:
- **Business** (strategy, revenue, market)
- **Technology** (engineering, architecture, technical constraints)
- **Users** (needs, pain points, experience)

## Vai trò cốt lõi

### 1. Product Vision & Strategy
- Định hướng sản phẩm dài hạn
- Xác định market positioning
- Competitive analysis
- Value proposition

### 2. Discovery & Research
- User research
- Market research
- Problem validation
- Opportunity assessment

### 3. Planning & Roadmapping
- Product roadmap
- Release planning
- Feature prioritization
- Resource allocation

### 4. Execution & Delivery
- Requirements definition
- Sprint planning
- Backlog management
- Stakeholder communication

### 5. Analytics & Iteration
- Metrics tracking
- User feedback analysis
- A/B testing
- Continuous improvement

## PM vs Product Owner

| Aspect | Product Manager | Product Owner |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Scope | Strategic + Tactical | Tactical |
| Focus | Product success | Sprint delivery |
| Timeline | Quarters/Years | Sprints/Months |
| Stakeholders | External + Internal | Mainly internal |
| Decisions | What to build & why | How to build |

## Essential Skills

### Hard Skills
1. **Data Analysis** - SQL, Analytics tools, Metrics
2. **Technical Understanding** - APIs, Architecture, Tech stack
3. **UX/UI Basics** - User flows, Wireframing, Design thinking
4. **Business Acumen** - P&L, Unit economics, Market dynamics
5. **Tools** - JIRA, Figma, Analytics platforms

### Soft Skills
1. **Communication** - Clear, concise, persuasive
2. **Leadership** - Influence without authority
3. **Decision Making** - Data-driven + intuition
4. **Empathy** - User-centric thinking
5. **Collaboration** - Cross-functional teamwork

## PM trong môi trường AI

Modern PMs cần:
- Hiểu AI capabilities và limitations
- Leverage AI tools cho research và analysis
- Design AI-powered features
- Manage AI ethics và bias
- Collaborate với ML engineers

## Frameworks phổ biến

1. **RICE Scoring** - Prioritization
2. **AARRR (Pirate Metrics)** - Growth
3. **Jobs-to-be-Done** - User needs
4. **OKRs** - Goal setting
5. **Product-Market Fit** - Validation

## Nguyên tắc thành công

1. **Be User-Obsessed** - Luôn đặt user lên hàng đầu
2. **Data-Informed, Not Data-Driven** - Dữ liệu hỗ trợ, không thay thế judgment
3. **Say No More Than Yes** - Focus is key
4. **Ship Fast, Learn Fast** - Iteration over perfection
5. **Build Trust** - With team, stakeholders, users

## Challenges thường gặp

- **Scope Creep** - Feature bloat
- **Stakeholder Conflicts** - Competing priorities
- **Technical Debt** - Short-term vs long-term
- **Market Changes** - Rapid pivots needed
- **Resource Constraints** - Limited time/budget

## Career Path

```
Associate PM → PM → Senior PM → Lead PM → Director of Product → VP of Product → CPO
```
